2.8" display, stereo microphones, temperature/humidity, accelerometer and light 
sensors. Three RGB LEDs. 

IO's at the bottom PCB-edge connector and also in connectors at the side.
Speaker, micro SD card slot and battery charger.
2MP camera. 

Hardware specs:
https://www.unihiker.com/wiki/K10/HardwareReference/hardwarereference_specs/

Install Unihiker library from this link (download ".whl" file):
https://pypi.org/project/unihiker/#files . 
Inside the Thonny IDE go to Tools > Manages packages and click "", 
search for the ".whl" file you just downloaded. It takes a while to install.
